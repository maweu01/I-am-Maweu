# 💫About Me :
I am currently working on two projects: A professional web-based Aerial Survey Flight Planning and Geospatial Intelligence Platform built for survey companies, drone operators, GIS analysts, and land professionals in Kenya and East Africa, and a cloud-based Aircraft Maintenance Management and Fleet Intelligence Platform built for charter aviation operators in Kenya, aligned with KCAA, ICAO, and FAA airworthiness standards.
